Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2026

Zebra Game Studio built Reef Pipe Quest as a mobile puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected or processed when you use the app and how that information may be handled.

Information We Collect

Reef Pipe Quest does not require account registration and does not ask you to provide personal details such as your name, phone number, postal address, or email address inside the app.

The app may collect or process limited technical information related to your device, app installation, and operation of the game. This may include:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device identifiers and app installation identifiers
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Language, screen size, and device configuration
  • General app usage and interaction information
  • Crash logs and technical diagnostic information

How Information Is Used

Limited technical information may be used for analytics and diagnostic purposes, including to understand how the app is used, evaluate general app performance, maintain compatibility with supported devices, identify and resolve errors, improve stability, and support future technical improvements.

Device identifiers, app installation identifiers, and the Advertising ID may be used to distinguish app installations and support aggregated technical analytics.

These identifiers are not used by Reef Pipe Quest to display personalized advertisements because the app does not contain advertising.

Offline Functionality

Reef Pipe Quest is designed to work offline and does not require a continuous internet connection for normal gameplay.

Internet access may occasionally be used for limited technical analytics, diagnostics, platform-related functionality, or delivery of app updates through the app distribution platform.

Gameplay progress, unlocked levels, collected stars, best move results, hint availability, and app settings may be stored locally on your device. Removing the app or clearing its data may permanently delete locally stored progress.

Advertising

Reef Pipe Quest does not display third-party advertisements and does not use advertising services such as Google AdMob to show ads inside the app.

The Advertising ID is not used for personalized advertising, advertising profiling, ad targeting, or displaying advertisements. Where available, it may be processed only for limited technical analytics, installation measurement, security, and diagnostic purposes.

Purchases and Payments

Reef Pipe Quest does not include in-app purchases, paid subscriptions, or an in-game store involving real money.

The app does not collect or process payment card details, banking information, purchase history, or Google Play Billing information.

Gameplay and Progress Data

The app may store gameplay-related information locally on your device, including completed levels, unlocked levels, collected stars, best move counts, remaining hints, and selected settings.

This information is used to save your progress, display your results, unlock subsequent levels, and provide the normal functionality of the game.

Reef Pipe Quest does not require a cloud account and does not provide cloud synchronization of gameplay progress.

Crash Logs and Diagnostics

The app may generate crash logs and limited diagnostic information when an error or technical problem occurs.

This information may include the app version, device model, operating system version, error details, performance information, and the technical state of the app at the time of the problem.

Diagnostic information is used to understand technical problems, improve app stability, maintain compatibility, and help prevent similar errors in future versions.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account credentials or login information
  • No names, phone numbers, email addresses, or postal addresses
  • No precise or approximate location data
  • No payment card, banking, or purchase information
  • No contacts or address book information
  • No photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • No camera or microphone data
  • No personal messages or communication content
  • No files or documents stored on your device
  • No health, fitness, financial, or biometric information

Permissions

Reef Pipe Quest does not require access to your contacts, camera, microphone, precise location, photos, videos, or personal files for normal gameplay.

The app may use basic network access where necessary for limited analytics, diagnostics, security, app distribution services, or technical communication.

Third-Party Services

Reef Pipe Quest does not use Google AdMob or other third-party advertising services to display advertisements.

The app may rely on limited services provided by the device operating system, Google Play, or technical service providers for app distribution, installation measurement, analytics, diagnostics, security, and compatibility.

These service providers may process limited technical information such as device identifiers, Advertising ID, app installation identifiers, app version, device information, usage events, and crash data in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies.

Your device operating system and the app distribution platform may also independently process certain information. Such processing is controlled by those platform providers and is subject to their own privacy practices.

Data Sharing

Zebra Game Studio does not sell, rent, or trade users’ personal information.

Limited technical information may be processed by service providers that support analytics, diagnostics, security, app distribution, and technical operation. Such information is used only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Information may also be disclosed where required by applicable law, a valid legal request, or when reasonably necessary to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, Zebra Game Studio, or others.

Data Retention

Game progress and settings stored locally remain on your device until you delete the app, clear its storage, reset the relevant settings, or remove the data through controls provided by your device.

Limited analytics and diagnostic information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to analyze app performance, investigate technical issues, improve the app, satisfy legal obligations, and protect the app from misuse.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information associated with the app against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Reef Pipe Quest does not require account creation and does not intentionally request personal information from children.

Zebra Game Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that the matter can be reviewed and appropriate action can be taken.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights relating to access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection regarding personal information associated with you.

You may manage, reset, or delete your Android Advertising ID through the privacy settings available on your device.

You may delete locally stored gameplay data by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app. This action may permanently remove your level progress, stars, best results, and settings.

Because Reef Pipe Quest does not require an account, we may be unable to identify or connect technical information to a particular user without additional details about the device, app installation, or circumstances involved.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the app, technical functionality, legal requirements, or privacy practices.

Any updates will be published on this page together with a revised effective date. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
