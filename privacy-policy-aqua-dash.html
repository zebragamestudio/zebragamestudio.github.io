Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2026

Zebra Game Studio built Aqua Dash Tap as a mobile arcade game. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected or processed when you use the app and how that information may be handled.

Information We Collect

Aqua Dash Tap does not require account registration and does not ask you to provide personal details such as your name, phone number, postal address, or email address inside the app.

The app may collect or process limited technical information related to your device and the operation of the app. This may include:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device identifiers
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Language, screen size, and device configuration
  • Crash logs and technical diagnostic information

How Information Is Used

Limited technical information may be used to help the app function correctly, maintain compatibility with supported devices, identify and resolve errors, improve stability, protect the app from misuse, and support future technical improvements.

Device identifiers, including the Advertising ID where available, are not used by Aqua Dash Tap to display personalized advertisements because the app does not contain advertising.

Offline Functionality

Aqua Dash Tap is designed to work offline and does not require a continuous internet connection for normal gameplay.

Gameplay progress, settings, achievements, scores, and similar game information may be stored locally on your device. Removing the app or clearing its data may permanently delete locally stored progress.

Advertising

Aqua Dash Tap does not display third-party advertisements and does not use advertising services such as Google AdMob.

The app does not use the Advertising ID for personalized advertising, advertising profiling, or advertising measurement.

Purchases and Payments

Aqua Dash Tap does not include in-app purchases, paid subscriptions, or an in-game store involving real money.

The app does not collect or process payment card details, banking information, purchase history, or Google Play Billing information.

Crash Logs and Diagnostics

The app may generate crash logs and limited diagnostic information when an error occurs. This information may include the app version, device model, operating system version, error details, and the technical state of the app at the time of the problem.

Diagnostic information is used only to understand technical problems, improve app stability, maintain compatibility, and help prevent similar errors in future versions.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account credentials or login information
  • No names, phone numbers, email addresses, or postal addresses
  • No precise or approximate location data
  • No payment or purchase information
  • No contacts or address book information
  • No photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • No camera or microphone data
  • No personal messages or communication content
  • No files or documents stored on your device
  • No health, fitness, financial, or biometric information

Permissions

Aqua Dash Tap does not require access to your contacts, camera, microphone, precise location, photos, videos, or personal files for normal gameplay.

Third-Party Services

Aqua Dash Tap does not use Firebase, Google AdMob, AppMetrica, or other third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, behavioral tracking, or user profiling.

Your device operating system and the app distribution platform may independently process certain technical information according to their own terms and privacy policies. Such processing is controlled by those platform providers and not by Zebra Game Studio.

Data Sharing

Zebra Game Studio does not sell, rent, or trade users’ personal information. The app does not share personal information with advertisers, data brokers, analytics providers, or marketing companies.

Information may be disclosed only where required by applicable law, a valid legal request, or when reasonably necessary to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, Zebra Game Studio, or others.

Data Retention

Game progress and settings stored locally remain on your device until you delete the app, clear its storage, reset the relevant settings, or remove the data through controls provided by your device.

Any technical information received for troubleshooting purposes is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to investigate errors, improve the app, satisfy legal obligations, and protect the app from misuse.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information associated with the app. However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Aqua Dash Tap does not require account creation and does not intentionally request personal information from children.

Zebra Game Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that the matter can be reviewed.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights relating to access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection regarding personal information associated with you.

You may also manage or reset your Android Advertising ID through the privacy settings available on your device.

Because Aqua Dash Tap does not require an account, we may be unable to identify or connect technical information to a particular user without additional details about the device or circumstances involved.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the app, legal requirements, or privacy practices.

Any updates will be published on this page together with a revised effective date. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
