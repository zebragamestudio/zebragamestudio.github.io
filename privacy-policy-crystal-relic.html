Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

Zebra Game Studio built the Crystal Relic app as a mobile puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Crystal Relic does not require account registration and does not ask you to provide personal details such as your name, phone number, postal address, or email address inside the app.

The app may collect or process limited technical information related to your device and app usage. This may include:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device identifiers
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Language and screen configuration
  • Crash logs and technical diagnostic information
  • Basic information needed to display and manage advertising

How Information Is Used

Limited technical information may be used to help the app function correctly, maintain compatibility, identify and resolve technical issues, improve stability, and support advertising displayed in the app.

Advertising identifiers may be used to help provide relevant ads, measure advertising performance, prevent fraud, and limit repeated advertising where applicable.

Advertising

Crystal Relic displays advertisements. Advertising-related information, including the Advertising ID and certain device information, may be processed for the purpose of displaying, measuring, and improving ads.

You can manage or reset your Android Advertising ID through the privacy settings available on your device.

Crash Logs and Diagnostics

The app may collect crash logs and limited diagnostic information when an error occurs. This information is used only to understand technical problems, improve app stability, and help prevent similar issues in future versions.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account or login information
  • No names, phone numbers, email addresses, or postal addresses
  • No precise or approximate location data
  • No payment or purchase information
  • No contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access
  • No personal messages or files stored on your device

In-Game Store

Crystal Relic includes an in-game store where players may use virtual coins earned through gameplay and daily rewards. The app does not process real-money purchases, payment card details, or Google Play Billing transactions.

Third-Party Services

Crystal Relic does not use Firebase, AdMob, or third-party SDKs for analytics, crash reporting, or user tracking.

Data Sharing

Zebra Game Studio does not sell or rent your personal information. Limited technical and advertising-related information may be processed as necessary to provide advertising functionality and maintain the app's operation.

Children’s Privacy

Crystal Relic does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require account creation and does not intentionally request personal data from users.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to keep the app simple and privacy-conscious. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have privacy rights regarding information associated with your device or advertising identifier. You may manage your Advertising ID through your device settings. For privacy-related questions, you may also contact us by email.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with an updated effective date.
