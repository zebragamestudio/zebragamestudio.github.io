Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

Zebra Game Studio built the KenKenDoku app as an offline mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

KenKenDoku does not require account registration and does not request personal details such as your name, phone number, or email address inside the app.

The app may access limited device information that is necessary for the app to function properly on your device. This may include general technical information such as device type, operating system version, language settings, screen configuration, or similar non-personal technical data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account or login information
  • No precise or approximate location data
  • No payment or purchase information
  • No advertising identifiers for ad targeting
  • No analytics data collection through Firebase or similar services
  • No crash reporting through third-party SDKs
  • No personal contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access

Offline Use

KenKenDoku is designed to work offline. The app does not require an internet connection for normal gameplay.

Advertising

KenKenDoku does not display third-party advertisements.

Third-Party Services

KenKenDoku does not use Firebase and does not integrate third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or user tracking.

How Information Is Used

Any limited technical device information used by the app is processed only for basic app functionality, compatibility, and performance on your device.

Data Sharing

Zebra Game Studio does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

KenKenDoku does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal data, the risk of collecting such information is minimized.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the app simple and privacy-friendly. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have certain privacy rights regarding your information. If you have any questions about privacy or data handling, you can contact us by email.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
